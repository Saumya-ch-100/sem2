Main Info

Breed Name: Samoyed

Origin: Siberia (Russia)

Weight: 20 - 30 kg

Description

The Samoyed is a majestic cloud-like dog breed with a thick, white double coat and a smiley face. They’re known for their friendly personalities and their love for snow!

Origin

This ancient breed was developed by the Samoyede people of Siberia. They were used for herding reindeer, pulling sleds, and keeping their humans warm at night.

Personality

Samoyeds are sweet, loyal, and always look like they’re smiling! They are playful, social, and love being part of a family. But beware—they don’t like being alone for long!
