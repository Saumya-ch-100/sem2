🐆 Bengal Cat 🐾

Description

Mini leopards

Origin

Bengals were developed by crossing domestic cats with the Asian leopard cat in the 1970s. The goal was to create a cat with a wild look but a tame, loving nature.

Personality

Bengals are super active, smart, and playful. They love climbing, chasing, and playing with water! They're also very affectionate with their humans. 😻
